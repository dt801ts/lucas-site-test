    Secure contact

    Use the details below for ordinary correspondence and for messages that should be encrypted before they are sent. The PGP key lets you encrypt email to this site.

    PGP can protect message contents, but it does not hide that contact occurred, who contacted whom, when it happened, the subject line, or other email metadata.

    If your situation is sensitive or high-risk, pause before sending. Treat PGP email as one option, not a complete confidential channel; use a dedicated channel when one is available, and compare the fingerprint independently when the stakes are serious.
